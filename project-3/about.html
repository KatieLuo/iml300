About Page

The creator: My name is Katie Luo, and I am a student at USC studying Media Arts+Practice. I made this toolkit to address Asian American Representation in Western media.

This website serves as a toolkit with four parts: the problem with Asian American representation, the historical context, good examples of representation and conversation, and moving forward representation. The website is covered in hyper-links and some interactive windows, so please feel free to click anything that stands out!
